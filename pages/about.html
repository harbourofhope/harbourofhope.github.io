About Harbour of Hope

A place of compassion, hope, and healing for life's journey.

Welcome to Harbour of Hope

Harbour of Hope is a not-for-profit counselling ministry committed to walking alongside individuals, couples and families through life's challenges.

We believe that every person deserves to be heard with compassion, treated with dignity, and supported with hope.

Our aim is to provide a safe and confidential space where people can find clarity, encouragement and practical guidance as they navigate difficult seasons of life.

Our Mission

Bringing Hope Through Compassionate Care

We support people from all walks of life who may be experiencing:

  • Anxiety, stress and emotional struggles
  • Depression and feelings of hopelessness
  • Suicidal thoughts (encouraging professional support where appropriate)
  • Addictions, including social media addiction
  • Relationship and family challenges
  • Pre-marital and marital concerns
  • Grief and loss
  • Workplace stress and career transitions
  • Major life transitions and personal wellbeing
  • Guidance during seasons of uncertainty and change

Whether you're facing a specific challenge or simply need someone to listen, Harbour of Hope offers a place where you can begin your journey towards healing.

Our Approach

Confidential • Compassionate • Hope-Centred

Every counselling journey is unique. Sessions are conducted in a respectful, confidential and non-judgemental environment, allowing each person to explore their concerns at a pace that feels comfortable.

Harbour of Hope provides scheduled online counselling by appointment.

Meet

Esther Khatri

Esther Khatri is the Lead Counsellor at Harbour of Hope. With many years of experience as an educator and counsellor, she has journeyed alongside people from diverse backgrounds, helping them navigate emotional, relational and personal challenges with empathy, wisdom and care.

Her desire is to create a welcoming environment where every individual feels valued, heard and supported as they take steps towards healing and renewed hope.

Important Information

Harbour of Hope offers scheduled counselling appointments and is not an emergency crisis intervention service.

If you or someone you know is experiencing an immediate mental health crisis or is at risk of harm, please contact your local emergency services or an appropriate crisis support service without delay.
